## What is HaudioFeel?
A free music player that fetches the top songs from currated spotify playlists based on your mood!

## Check it out
Deployed at [haudiofeel.netlify.app](https://haudiofeel.netlify.app/)

## How to use?
Getting started is super simple üôè Click on any of the emojis to play music fitting to that feeling and cycle between tracks.
